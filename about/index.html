Remote ’n Active was born out of my passion for remote work and the growing trend of professionals embracing the freedom and flexibility it offers. As remote work became more prevalent, I noticed a crucial aspect missing from the conversation: the need for social connection and an active lifestyle.

I started this project to provide a platform where remote workers could find valuable resources, tips, and insights on maintaining an active and healthy lifestyle while working remotely. From work-from-home productivity tips to work-life balance strategies, Remote ’n Active aimed to bridge the gap between work and well-being.

However, as life takes us on unexpected adventures, I have decided to archive this project. Though I am no longer maintaining the website, I am grateful for the incredible journey it has been.

I want to express my gratitude to every subscriber, visitor, reader, and contributor who joined me on this ride. Your support and engagement have made Remote ’n Active a sparkling online space. It has been a pleasure to connect with like-minded individuals and witness the positive impact our shared experiences have had on each other’s lives.

While Remote ’n Active may no longer be actively updated, I encourage you to explore the archives and discover the wealth of information and inspiration it holds. Let the articles, guides, and stories continue to serve as a valuable resource for remote workers seeking a balanced and active lifestyle.

Thank you once again for being a part of this journey. Keep embracing the remote work lifestyle, staying active, and thriving in all your endeavors.

Wishing you all the best,

Jesse
