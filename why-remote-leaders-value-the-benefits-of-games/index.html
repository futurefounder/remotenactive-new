Why Remote Leaders Value The Benefits Of Games

If you’ve ever worked with a team that is scattered through different locations, you know the difficulties of collaborating remotely.

“Removing roadblocks to improve productivity is a key skillset of any remote manager.” - GitLab “Being a great remote manager”

The challenges of working with a distributed team may be facing different geographical time zones, cultural differences, bad WiFi connections, or simply the loud background noises of a subway because your colleague took the call on the go.

All this can create a big challenge for collaboration and productivity as a remote team.

But, fortunately, there is a way of learning how to deal with challenging situations.

You’ve guessed it.

Gaming.

One way to make things easier is by playing games together.

Whether it’s just for fun or to practice new skills games have a scientifically proven track record of increasing mental and emotional capabilities.

That is why you should give it a shot.

Here are some ideas for some great games that will help your remote team work together better.

But first, let’s talk about

⚡ Benefits of playing games

Gaming is getting a lot of attention as a way to loosen the mind and unwind quickly.

Playing games gives you a distraction from your work, so you can focus on what needs to be done in the game you are playing.

It can be relaxing, but it can also help you focus because you are taking control of an avatar and having fun differently than how you might be doing it at work.

It can be difficult to find time to play video games with your team when you feel like you’re doing a lot of work.

But it is really beneficial.

🧠 The psychology behind online gaming

Video games can help build teamwork.

They can help you relax after a hard day at work.

They can give you a sense of achievement when you beat a difficult challenge.

A lot of people who want to work for game makers or software developers say that playing games helps them relax and focus better.

Others believe that socializing in online games can help improve communication skills because people are forced to talk about their experiences with one another instead of feeling like they are stuck or awkward.

Popular games offer team-building activities and real-time chat functions that make meetings easier to manage.

And as the cost of regional Internet access has dropped to marginal levels, gaming has become an accessible form of recreation for millions of people around the world.

Takeaway: Why should you play games?

Games are fun and exciting additions to any busy person’s busy day!

They help you unwind after a long day and allows you to bond with your co-workers over a common interest or cause.

There are various types of online games available today including competitive games, action sports games, and even role-playing games.

All these games provide amazing entertainment for you.

The best part is, you don’t need to be an expert gamer to enjoy any of them.
